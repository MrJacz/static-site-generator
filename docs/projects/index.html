Impact - Reduced energy consumption by 40% across participating data centers - Prevented 150,000 tons of CO2 emissions in its first year - Winner of the Global Green Tech Award 2023

A non-invasive BCI system that allows direct interaction between human thoughts and computer systems. Currently in beta testing with select research institutions.
